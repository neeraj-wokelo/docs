akta.pro's APIs are priced in credits. Each API consumes a set number of credits per call; the sections below break down credit consumption by API type.

## Cost per credit and rate limits

The cost per credit and rate limit depend on your plan:

| Plans | Pay as you go | Subscription | Enterprise |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cost per credit | \$0.05 | \$0.04 | Custom |
| Rate Limit (requests / min) | 100 | 200 | Custom |

## Guidelines and notes

- Top-up credits are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.
- Monthly plan credits expire at the end of each billing cycle and do not roll over.
- Credits are non-transferable between accounts and non-redeemable for cash.
