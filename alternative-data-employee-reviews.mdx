---
title: "Overview"
description: "Fetch a company's aggregated employee review signals — overall rating, eight dimension-level scores, and paginated individual reviews with pros, cons, reviewer metadata, and per-dimension ratings — sourced from Glassdoor and other review providers."
---

The Employee Reviews API returns aggregated workplace sentiment data and individual employee reviews for any company. A single request returns the overall company rating, eight dimension-level scores (culture, work-life balance, compensation, senior management, diversity and inclusion, business outlook, CEO approval, and recommendation rate), and a paginated list of individual reviews — each with the reviewer's pros, cons, job title, tenure, employment status, location, and per-dimension ratings.

This is a **synchronous GET API** — pass a company identifier and optional pagination parameters and receive the full response immediately.

<Info>
  This API is synchronous. Results are returned directly in the HTTP response — no job submission or polling required.
</Info>
