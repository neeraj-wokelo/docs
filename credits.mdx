## Summary

Akta's APIs are priced in credits. Cost depends on your plan, endpoint, and monthly credit tier.

| Plan | Cost per credit | Best for |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Pay as you go | \$\$\$ | Getting started without commitment |
| Starter | \$\$ | Light developer and workflow usage |
| Pro | \$ | Production and higher-volume usage |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom endpoints, feeds, limits, and deployment needs |

<Info>
  Higher plans get lower per-credit pricing and higher rate limits.
</Info>

## Credits

Your credit balance is displayed at the top of the billing page. Add credits or subscribe to a plan from the billing page.

## Company News API

The Company News endpoint uses a fixed credit cost per request plus a variable cost based on results returned.

**Credit formula:**

$$
\text{credits used} = 0.1 + (0.01 \times \text{number of results})
$$

| Plan | Price (\$) | Calculation |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Pay as you go | 8.0 / 1k requests\* | Calculated at \$0.040 / credit |
| Starter | 7.2 / 1k requests\* | Calculated at \$0.036 / credit |
| Pro | 6.4 / 1k requests\* | Calculated at \$0.032 / credit |
| Enterprise | Custom | Based on committed volume, credits, endpoints, and rate limits |

<Note>
  The listed Search prices are calculated for requests returning up to 10 results.
</Note>

## Guidelines and notes

- Top-up credits are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.
- Monthly plan credits expire at the end of each billing cycle and do not roll over.
- Credits are non-transferable between accounts and non-redeemable for cash.
