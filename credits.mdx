## Account balance

Your remaining balance is displayed at the top of the billing page along with options to top up credits or add a payment method.

A payment method must be added via **Setup** before top-ups and recurring billing can be processed.

## Pay as you go pricing

Akta usage is charged in credits. The cost per request depends on your plan's per-credit rate.

| **Plan** | **Endpoint** | **Price** | **Calculation** |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Pay as you go | Search | \$8.00 / 1k requests\* | Calculated at \$0.04 / credit. |
| Starter | Search | \$7.20 / 1k requests\* | Calculated at \$0.036 / credit. |
| Pro | Search | \$6.40 / 1k requests\* | Calculated at \$0.032 / credit. |
| Enterprise | Search | Custom | Custom pricing based on committed volume, credits, endpoints, and rate limits. |

<Info>
  \*0.1 credits fixed \+ 0.01 credit for every result. Calculated for up to 10 results per request. 
</Info>

## Guidelines and Notes

- Top up your balance at any time using the **Top up balance** action.
- A payment method must be added before top-ups can be processed.
- Higher-tier plans offer discounted per-credit pricing and increased rate limits.
- Within Starter and Pro, you can scale credits up or down at the next billing cycle.
- Plan upgrades take effect immediately; remaining balance carries over.
- For pricing discussions or custom plan adjustments, please contact [**sales@wokelo.ai**](mailto:sales@wokelo.ai).
