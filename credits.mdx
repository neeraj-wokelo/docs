## Summary

Akta usage is charged in credits. Pricing depends on your plan, endpoint usage, and monthly credit tier.

| Plan | Price | Best for | Search | Rate limit | Support |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Pay as you go | \$ | Getting started without commitment | \$ | 50 requests / minute | Email support |
| Starter | \$ | Light developer and workflow usage | \$ | 100 requests / minute | Email support |
| Pro | \$\$ | Production and higher-volume usage | \$ | 250 requests / minute | Priority support |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom endpoints, feeds, limits, and deployment needs | Custom | Custom | Dedicated Account Manager |

<Info>
  Search pricing is calculated using credits. Higher plans receive lower per-credit pricing and higher rate limits.
</Info>

## Credits

Your remaining credit balance is displayed at the top of the billing page.

You can top up credits or add a payment method from the billing page. A payment method must be added via **Setup** before top-ups or recurring billing can be processed.

## Search API

The Search endpoint is priced using a fixed credit cost per request plus a variable credit cost based on the number of results returned.

| Component | Credit usage |
| :-- | :-- |
| Fixed request cost | 0.1 credits |
| Per result returned | 0.01 credits |

**Credit formula**

$$
\text{credits used} = 0.1 + (0.01 \times \text{number of results})
$$

<Note>
  The listed Search prices are calculated for requests returning up to 10 results.
</Note>

## Plans

Starter and Pro plans include multiple monthly credit tiers at the same per-credit rate. Credits and monthly price scale together, while rate limits, support level, and per-credit pricing remain consistent within each plan.

|  | Pay as you go | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |

| Best for | Minimum commitment | Light developer usage | Heavy production usage | Custom enterprise needs |
| Monthly price | No monthly subscription | From \$18 / month | From \$128 / month | Custom |
| Included credits | Top up as needed | 500–2,500 credits | 4,000–15,000 credits | Custom |

## Guidelines and notes

- You can top up your balance at any time using **Top up balance**.
- A payment method must be added before top-ups can be processed.
- Higher-tier plans receive discounted per-credit pricing and increased rate limits.
- Starter and Pro customers can scale credit tiers up or down at the next billing cycle.
- Plan upgrades take effect immediately, and remaining balance carries over.
- Enterprise plans can include custom endpoints, de-duplicated feeds, custom limits, and dedicated account support.
