# akta.pro API Documentation

## Docs

- [Employee Reviews API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/employee-reviews.md): Fetches a company's employee review data including overall ratings, dimension-level ratings (culture, work-life balance, senior management and more) and individual employee reviews.
- [Headcount Trends API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/headcount-trends.md): Fetches a company's LinkedIn-sourced headcount data, including total employee count, historical growth trends, and a functional breakdown of the workforce.
- [Job Posts API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/job-posts.md): Fetches live LinkedIn and Indeed job listings for a company, including title, location, description, compensation, experience level, and extracted key skills.
- [Product Reviews API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/product-reviews.md): Fetches a company's product catalog and detailed reviews per product including ratings, star distribution, pros, cons, pricing and individual review content.
- [Social Posts API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/social-posts.md): Fetches social media posts published by a company, including content type, text, publish date, paid/repost flags, an AI classification, and detailed engagement metrics.
- [Website Traffic API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/website-traffic.md): Fetches a company's website traffic signals, including engagement metrics, historical monthly visit estimates, and a breakdown of traffic by acquisition source.
- [Company Data API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/company-data.md): Enriches a company with firmographics, business model, headcount, traffic, funding, financials, acquisitions and investment data.
- [News Signals API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/news-signals.md): Fetches news articles enriched with AI-generated summaries, event types, publisher metadata, sentiment, industry tags, company mentions and full article text.
- [Company Search API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/supporting-apis/company-search.md): Resolves a company by name, website or UUID and returns core identity and classification fields.
- [Industry Search API](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/supporting-apis/industry-search.md): Resolves an industry from a free-text query and returns matching industry codes ranked by similarity.
- [Changelog](https://docs.akta.pro/changelog.md): New features, enhancements, and updates to the Akta API.
