# akta.pro

> akta.pro provides a Private Markets API-suite for Agents: enterprise-grade private company data, finally made accessible to AI. Use the API to enrich private company data, monitor company and industry news, and capture alternative signals across 20M+ entity-resolved companies globally. All endpoints are synchronous GET APIs authenticated with an `x-api-key` header.

## News Signals API

- [Overview](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/news-signals/overview.md): Fetch news articles enriched with AI summaries, event types, sentiment, industry tags, named entities, and company mentions, scoped by company, industry, open-ended query, or any other filter.
- [Quick Start](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/news-signals/quick-start.md): Make your first News Signals call; all filters are optional and open-ended query is supported.
- [Use cases](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/news-signals/use-cases.md): Company monitoring, industry intelligence, open-ended topic monitoring, and entity tracking.
- [Best Practices](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/news-signals/best-practices.md): Resolving industry codes, open-ended query and entity filters, deduplication, and pagination.
- [API reference](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/news-signals.md): GET /v1/news — full request parameters and response fields including entities and classification codes.

## Alternative Signals

### Employee Reviews API

- [Overview](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/employee-reviews/overview.md): Aggregated employee review signals — overall rating, eight dimension-level scores, and paginated individual reviews with pros, cons, and reviewer metadata, sourced from Glassdoor and other providers.
- [Quick Start](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/employee-reviews/quick-start.md): Make your first Employee Reviews call and read overall and dimension-level ratings.
- [Use cases](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/employee-reviews/use-cases.md): Aggregate sentiment snapshots, reading individual reviews, and competitive benchmarking.
- [API reference](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/employee-reviews.md): Overall ratings, dimension-level ratings (culture, work-life balance, senior management, and more), and individual employee reviews.

### Product Reviews API

- [Overview](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/product-reviews/overview.md): A company's full product catalog and, optionally, detailed per-product reviews — ratings, star distributions, pros and cons, pricing, and individual review content sourced from G2 and other providers.
- [Quick Start](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/product-reviews/quick-start.md): Make your first Product Reviews call and read a company's product catalog and ratings.
- [Use cases](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/product-reviews/use-cases.md): Aggregate rating snapshots, reading individual reviews, and competitive benchmarking across products.
- [Best Practices](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/product-reviews/best-practices.md): Handling rating scales, null fields, pagination, and separating verified from unverified reviews.
- [API reference](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/product-reviews.md): Product catalog and detailed reviews per product including ratings, star distribution, pros, cons, pricing, and individual review content.

### Headcount Trends API

- [Overview](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/headcount-trends/overview.md): LinkedIn-sourced headcount — total employee count, historical growth trends, and a functional breakdown of the workforce.
- [Quick Start](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/headcount-trends/quick-start.md): Make your first Headcount Trends call and read total employees and growth rates.
- [Use cases](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/headcount-trends/use-cases.md): Growth snapshots, historical trend charting, function-level breakdown, and competitive benchmarking.
- [API reference](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/headcount-trends.md): Total employee count, historical growth trends, and a functional breakdown of the workforce.

### Website Traffic API

- [Overview](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/website-traffic/overview.md): Website traffic signals — engagement metrics, historical monthly visit estimates, and a breakdown of traffic by acquisition source.
- [Quick Start](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/website-traffic/quick-start.md): Make your first Website Traffic call and read visits and engagement metrics.
- [Use cases](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/website-traffic/use-cases.md): Engagement snapshots, trend analysis, traffic channel mix, and competitive benchmarking.
- [API reference](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/website-traffic.md): Engagement metrics, historical monthly visit estimates, and a breakdown of traffic by acquisition source.

### Job Posts API

- [Overview](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/job-posts/overview.md): Live LinkedIn and Indeed job listings — title, location, description, compensation, experience level, and extracted key skills.
- [Quick Start](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/job-posts/quick-start.md): Make your first Job Posts call and list a company's open roles.
- [Use cases](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/job-posts/use-cases.md): Open roles snapshots, hiring by function and location, skills demand analysis, and compensation benchmarking.
- [API reference](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/job-posts.md): Live LinkedIn and Indeed job listings including title, location, description, compensation, experience level, and extracted key skills.

### Social Posts API

- [Overview](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/social-posts/overview.md): Social media posts published by a company — content type, text, publish date, paid/repost flags, an AI classification, and detailed engagement metrics.
- [Quick Start](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/social-posts/quick-start.md): Make your first Social Posts call and read post content and engagement.
- [Use cases](https://docs.akta.pro/docs/alternative-data/social-posts/use-cases.md): Content mix by classification and format, engagement performance, and posting cadence.
- [API reference](https://docs.akta.pro/api-reference/alternative-data/social-posts.md): Social media posts including content type, text, publish date, paid/repost flags, an AI classification, and detailed engagement metrics.
